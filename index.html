How to Play

  1. Press the play button, a series of numbered box(es) will flash.
  2. After the numbered box(es) have finished flashing, it is your turn to replicate the pattern.
  3. If you have correctly replicated the pattern you will move on to the next level.
  4. If you didn't replicate the pattern you loose, and the game will end.
  5. To play again, simply press the "Reset" buton.
